Your Privacy

Cookies are pieces of data stored on your browser or device that help us provide you a smooth and personalized experience on our web properties. We use different types of cookies, which are outlined below. ​
More information

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Always Active

Strictly Necessary cookies are vital for our (Marriott) property's core functionality, such as enabling booking systems, secure payment processes, and navigation through our hotel's amenities pages.

Functional Cookies

Active

Functional cookies remember user preferences, like language settings, room choices, and past reservations, enhancing user experience by personalizing interactions on our site.

Advertising Cookies

Active

Advertising cookies (also known as Marketing cookies) allow Marriott to log user interactions to deliver targeted ads and promotions, showcasing special offers, events, or loyalty programs.

Analytics Cookies

Active

Analytics Cookies help us improve our websites and mobile apps by collecting and reporting information on how you use them without directly identifying you.
